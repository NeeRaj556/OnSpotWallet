## 🔐 Balance Privacy Feature

### Hidden by Default

Your balance is **hidden for privacy** when you open the app:

```
Balance                   👁  🔄  🚪

         X X X X X
```

### Tap Eye to Reveal

Tap the 👁 icon to see your actual balance:

```
Balance                   👁  🔄  🚪

       $100.00
```

### Tap Again to Hide

Tap the 👁 icon again to hide your balance:

```
Balance                   👁  🔄  🚪

       X X X X X
```

**Why This Matters:**

- 🔒 Privacy in public places
- 🛡️ Security from shoulder surfing
- 👀 Control who sees your finances

## 📷 QR Code Scanner

### Floating Scan Button

The **SCAN** button floats at the bottom center:

```
              ┌─────────────┐
              │  📷 SCAN   │
              └─────────────┘
```

### Two Modes

#### 1. Scanner Mode

- Open camera to scan QR codes
- Pay vendors, friends, or services
- Quick and secure

#### 2. My QR Mode

- Show your personal QR code
- Let others scan to pay you
- Share your wallet address

## 💡 Tips & Tricks

### Quick Actions

- **👁 Eye Icon**: Toggle balance visibility
- **🔄 Refresh Icon**: Reload your balance
- **🚪 Logout Icon**: Sign out of your account
- **📷 Scan Button**: Quick access to QR scanner

### Status Indicators

- **🛜 ONLINE**: Connected to internet (max $1000 transactions)
- **📴 OFFLINE**: BLE Mesh mode (max $10 transactions)

### Security Features

- Balance hidden by default
- Mounted state checks prevent crashes
- Secure payment confirmations
- Biometric support (coming soon)
